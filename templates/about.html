iRead.Kindle

Share Your Highlights to Weibo

iRead.Kindle is inspired by kindle.io, it can record all your Kindle highlights, which you share with friends using your social network accounts (like Twitter or Facebook).
However, both Twitter & Facebook could not be visited inside the FUCKING G.F.W, so it is necesary to provide a substitution for them, Weibo.
Read more and set up on the profile page, then your Highlights will show up at highlights page.

Setting steps are:

  1. Set a Twitter account as your Kindle Share Social Network.
  2. HighLight some text while you are reading on Kindle, and, Share it.
  3. As a result, Amazon will save these on your kindle profile page, which is PUBLIC for every Kindle reader.
  4. Type your own kindle profile page URL on profile page. And allow iRead.Kindle to update your Weibo.
  5. Then iRead.Kindle will fetch your Kindle Profile Page for latest highlights and share them to your Weibo account.
